If you have any questions about this policy or your data, you can contact us at:

Email: info@kalmkatz.co.uk

---

## 2. What Data We Collect

When you visit our website, we collect limited technical information via JavaScript and secure server logging. This may include:

- IP address
- Date and time of visit
- Browser type and version
- Operating system
- Referring website or source (where you came from)
- Pages accessed
- Approximate geographic location (derived from IP address)
- Technical indicators used to determine whether a visitor is human, automated, or potentially malicious

We do **not** use cookies.
We do **not** collect names, contact details, or account information unless you separately provide them to us.

---

## 3. Why We Collect This Data

We process this information for the following purposes:

- **Analytics** – to understand how visitors find our website and which marketing campaigns are effective
- **Business intelligence** – to determine general geographic demand and identify legitimate customer regions
- **Security monitoring** – to detect bots, attackers, and fraudulent activity
- **Abuse prevention and fraud detection** – to protect our website and infrastructure

We are not interested in identifying individual visitors. Our focus is understanding traffic sources and protecting our systems.

---

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process this data under **Legitimate Interests** (Article 6(1)(f) UK GDPR).

Our legitimate interests are:

- Operating and improving our business
- Measuring marketing effectiveness
- Protecting our website from misuse, fraud, and malicious activity

We believe this processing is proportionate, minimal, and does not override your fundamental rights and freedoms.

---

## 5. Data Retention

Raw technical logs are retained for a limited period for analytics and security purposes.

After this period, data is either:

- Automatically deleted, or
- Anonymised so that it can no longer identify any individual

Anonymised statistical data may be retained longer for business analysis.

## 6. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell your data.
We do **not** share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data is processed on our secured VPS infrastructure. Access is restricted and protected using appropriate technical security measures.

## 7. International Transfers

If you wish to exercise any of your rights, please contact us at:
